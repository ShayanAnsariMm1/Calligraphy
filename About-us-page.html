History

Calligraphy is like making words extra pretty. Long ago, in China, they used special brushes to make writing look like art. Other places like Japan and the Middle East also started doing cool writing stuff. Islamic calligraphy has fancy designs, especially in religious writings. In Japan, it's called "shodo," and it's all about simple writing tied to Zen Buddhism. Even in the Western world, people started making writing look fancy during the Renaissance. So, calligraphy is just about making words look really, really nice, and different places have their own fun ways of doing it!
